# Product Overview - Baseer

## What is Baseer?

Baseer (بصير) is an intelligent financial operating system designed specifically for the Arabic market, starting with Saudi Arabia. It's a comprehensive financial platform that combines invoice management, ZATCA compliance, customer management, and AI-powered financial insights into a unified solution.

## Core Value Proposition

- **ZATCA Compliance First**: Seamless e-invoicing compliance with Saudi Arabia's tax authority
- **AI-Powered Automation**: 90% reduction in manual financial tasks through intelligent automation
- **Arabic-Native Experience**: Built from the ground up for Arabic users with RTL support and cultural context
- **Unified Platform**: Single solution for both individual and business financial management

## Target Users

### Primary Segment
- Small business owners and freelancers in Saudi Arabia
- 500,000+ potential users
- Pain points: ZATCA compliance complexity, manual invoice management, lack of financial insights

### Secondary Segment  
- Tech-savvy individuals across GCC countries
- 2M+ potential users
- Pain points: Fragmented financial accounts, manual expense tracking, lack of financial planning tools

## Key Features

1. **Smart Invoice Management**: Automated invoice creation, ZATCA submission, and customer management
2. **Receipt Scanning**: OCR-powered expense tracking through mobile app
3. **Financial Analytics**: Real-time insights and predictive analytics
4. **Multi-platform Access**: Web, mobile (Flutter), and PWA support
5. **Secure & Compliant**: Bank-level security with full regulatory compliance

## Business Model

- Freemium SaaS model
- Target: 50,000 SAR/month by Q4 2025
- Focus on SME market with potential for GCC expansion
